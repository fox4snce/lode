About Lode

Lode helps you organize and search through your AI conversation history.

Features

  • Import: Import conversations from OpenAI (ChatGPT) and Claude
  • Search: Full-text search across all conversations and messages
  • Organize: Tag, star, bookmark, and add notes to conversations
  • Find Tools: Quickly find code blocks, links, TODOs, questions, dates, decisions, and prompts
  • Analytics: View usage statistics, streaks, top words, and more
  • Export: Export conversations to Markdown, CSV, or JSON

Version
